# Hiphop Gesture System

Use this reference when creating hiphop, rap, streetwear, underground street, or music-video style AIGC videos.

## Purpose

Hiphop gestures should strengthen attitude and tribe identity. They are not random dance decoration. Use them as repeated style anchors, especially when the product is subtle and the film sells atmosphere.

## Recommended Gesture Anchors

1. Head and shoulder:
   - slow head nod to the beat
   - slight chin lift toward camera
   - shoulder bounce, very restrained
   - slow side glance, then look away

2. Hands and arms:
   - one hand to chest on a beat
   - open-palm rap gesture toward lens
   - two-finger point downward or sideways
   - arms crossed, relaxed not stiff
   - hands in pockets with dropped shoulders
   - chain touch or necklace adjustment
   - hood, collar, or cap adjustment

3. Body pose:
   - wall lean with one knee bent
   - low squat, elbows resting on knees
   - wide stance, weight shifted to one leg
   - back turned to camera, then slight head turn
   - slow step forward, sneaker drag, or toe tap

4. Group language:
   - crew standing in a loose triangle
   - one person foreground, others blurred behind
   - synchronized head nods without full choreography
   - slow camera orbit around a still group
   - staggered poses that feel like one tribe

## Prompt Phrases

- restrained hiphop hand gestures
- classic rap-performance hand movement
- subtle head nod to the beat
- relaxed arms crossed pose
- hands in pockets, dropped shoulders
- slow chain adjustment
- low squat streetwear pose
- back-turn reveal with slight head glance
- loose crew formation under a bridge
- controlled shoulder bounce, no exaggerated dancing

## What To Avoid

- accidental gang signs
- weapon gestures
- offensive hand signs
- overacting or fake toughness
- constant fast hand motion
- exaggerated TikTok dance choreography
- random luxury props that overpower the clothes and atmosphere
- hands too close to camera if the model struggles with fingers

## Usage Rule

For one 30-45 second atmosphere film, choose 3-5 gesture anchors and repeat them across models. The repetition makes the film feel like one tribe instead of unrelated AI clips.
